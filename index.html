Out of Stock

Team manager, narrative designer, UI/UX artist

A 3D project developed by me and four other partners for our Master's 3D project class. Players, while infiltrated in a supermarket, will have to fend for their lives as they send customers away to their uncle's supermarket across town, any way they can - morally correct of not. Developed using the Unity game engine, Blender, Figma, FL Studio and FMOD.

Where's Raury?

Team manager, lead narrative designer, lead game programmer

A narrative experiment made by me and four partners. A text adventure style game where the player will have to find Raury - a missing friend - in their home town. However, the search for Raury will not be as straight forward as it could be, taking the player to the most unexpected destinations. Developed using the Inform7 engine and Krita.

Click here to try it out!

Echoes From Below

Team manager, lead narrative designer, animation programmer, support game programmer

A 2D game project developed by me and three partners for our Master's 2D game class. The player gets lost during a tour through the Paris Catacombs, being stuck thousands of feet under Paris. To find the exit, players must take on different mosters and challenges, revealing the truth about the place they've been drawn into. Developed using the Unity game engine, Procreate, and Aesprite.
